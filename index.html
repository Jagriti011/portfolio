Project 1

Interactive Exoplanet Explorer

-Problem: How can we create an intuitive, visually captivating, and interactive experience that simplifies complex planet data and fosters a sense of wonder for imaginary worlds?


-Imagine exploring distant, unchartered worlds with a simple touch. This project is an interactive 3D planet explorer designed to make learning about imaginary celestial bodies engaging and accessible for curious minds


-This is a personal UI/UX case study aimed at honing my skills in interactive prototyping, visual design, and user-centered thinking.


-My role: UI/UX Designer, Prototyper.


-Started with rough sketches and low-fidelity wireframes in Figma to explore different layouts and interaction patterns without getting bogged down by visual details.


-Utilized Figma's advanced prototyping capabilities to bring the experience to life, focusing on smooth transitions and engaging interactions.

Project 2

Voice-Activated Assistant

-Imagine interacting with your UI not just with touch, but with your voice. This project is a high-fidelity ProtoPie prototype demonstrating a voice-activated intelligent assistant, designed for intuitive hands-free interaction


- Leveraged ProtoPie's sophisticated capabilities to integrate real-time voice input and output, creating a truly interactive VUI experience.
